# 04. Information Gathering

To properly gather structural system requirements for SubWise, the following methodology was used:
1.  **Market Landscape Audit:** Examined standard commercial subscription cycles (weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual intervals) along with common trial limits.
2.  **User Surveys:** Distributed digital questionnaires to gauge personal tracking habits and note exactly how often users face unintended billing.
3.  **Functional Analysis:** Evaluated digital banking applications to identify feature gaps. Traditional bank statements only track *historical* expenses; they fail to project *future* cumulative financial burn rates.
