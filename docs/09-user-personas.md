# 09. User Persona

* **Name:** Alex, 22-year-old Student & Freelancer
* **Behaviors:** Frequently registers for premium asset packs, media streaming lines, and cloud hosting tiers.
* **Pain Points:** Struggles to keep tabs on varying billing dates. Often experiences unexpected account overdrafts due to overlapping annual subscription renewals.
* **Goals:** Needs a centralized dashboard that summarizes monthly financial outlays and clearly emphasizes which services are renewing next.
