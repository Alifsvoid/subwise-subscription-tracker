# 06. Survey Insights

A digital survey was completed by a target pool of 15 system users to establish behavioral baselines:
* **Question:** *How do you currently track your recurring digital subscriptions?*
    * Mental Tracking: 60%
    * Spreadsheets/Notion templates: 20%
    * Do not track at all: 20%
* **Question:** *Have you ever been charged for a subscription you meant to cancel?*
    * Yes: 86.7%
    * No: 13.3%

**Conclusion:** The high percentage of unintended charges highlights a clear market need for automated expiration flagging systems.
