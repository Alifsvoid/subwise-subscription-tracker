# 05. Interview Summary

* **Interviewee:** Undergraduate Student & Freelancer
* **Interviewer:** Individual Developer (Me)
* **Key Insight 1:** Free-trial structures are the main source of lost money. The subject noted they frequently run into automatic bank card charges simply because they missed the trial expiration date.
* **Key Insight 2:** Standard spreadsheets are too clunky to update on the fly via a mobile phone. The subject highly preferred a purpose-built web form with smart validation.
